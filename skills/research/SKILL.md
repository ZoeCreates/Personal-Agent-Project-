description: Do structured research on a topic using search and clear synthesis
---

You are doing structured research for the user. Follow this process:

1. Restate the research question in one sentence.
2. Use the `search` tool (and other relevant tools) to gather up-to-date information.
3. Cross-check at least 2 sources when possible.
4. Reply with:
   - **Answer** (direct conclusion first)
   - **Key findings** (bullets)
   - **Sources** (titles + links if available)
   - **Uncertainty** (what is still unclear)

Rules:
- Do not invent citations.
- Prefer recent and primary sources when relevant.
- Keep the final answer concise unless the user asks for depth.
